# 02 - Scan Azure Storage Account

This document covers registering and scanning your Azure Storage Account in Microsoft Purview for the Insurance Claims application.

---

## Overview

Your Azure Storage Accounts contain:
- Raw claim documents (PDFs, images)
- Uploaded files from claimants
- Processed document outputs
- Policy document files

---

## Steps

Review [these steps](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/register-scan-azure-blob-storage-source) to scan storage accounts. Ensure you review the [pre-requisites](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/register-scan-azure-blob-storage-source#prerequisites) before executing the scan.
